Ricci Flow

9:00am Parabolic Rescaling
For detailed explanation, click here.

Suppose \((M, g_t)\) is a Ricci flow on \(M\times I\). Let \(t\in [t_0-r^2,t_0]\subset I\) and \(\tilde{g}(\tilde{t})\) be the Ricci flow such that the following holds:

\(\tilde{t}\in [-1,0]\); \(\tilde{g}(\tilde{t})=g(t)\); and \( \partial_{\tilde{t}} \tilde{g}(\tilde{t}) = -2 Ric_{\tilde{g}(\tilde{t})}\).

Then we have \[\tilde{g}(\tilde{t})=\frac{1}{r^2}g(t_0 + r^2 \tilde{t}).\]
